FAQs_

How big can my team be?

The maximum team size allowed is 3 for Software Hackathon and GeoCaching Game, 2 for Ideathon. For remaining competitions the participation would be inidividual.

How would I get the instruction for the competitions?

You just need to register for the entire event with the available link once. The further details regarding each competition will be posted on our social media as the event goes on.

When will it start?

Vercera 2023 will stretch from 18th February to 23rd February 2023.

Where will it be conducted?

Vercera will be conducted at Aligarh Muslim University

Will there be food arrangement?

Oh-Yeah-Absolutely. You will be provided with meals on the competition days. Thanks to our sponsors.

Do I need to register for each event seperately?

No! It only costs Rupees 150 to be a registered member of the entire event. After registration, you can participate in any competition as you like.

How can we register as teams?

The team formation form will be shared one day before the respective competition.

What do I bring with me to the event?

For every competition, you need to bring your college ID. Rest depends on the requirements of the particular event.

Where do I submit my blog for the blog competition?

The blog theme and the submission link would be shared on the competition day.

Where do I give the coding contest

You will be shared the contest link at the competition day

What is the eligibility criteria?

You must be a student from any recognised University/College.
